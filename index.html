Why device heritage matters.

Organizations increasingly need to explain not only what devices they use, but where those devices come from and why they should be trusted.

01

Complex supply chains

Hardware products are assembled from many suppliers, regions, chips, modules, firmware versions, and hidden dependencies.

02

Procurement pressure

Security, compliance, and procurement teams need practical answers before devices are deployed into sensitive environments.

03

Trust must be explainable

A clear heritage map helps translate technical findings into business, regulatory, and operational decisions.
